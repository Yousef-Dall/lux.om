# PMS company onboarding guide

This checklist helps a property company move from an empty PMS workspace to beta-ready operations.

## Step 1: Confirm workspace access

- Log in with the PMS owner account.
- Open `/pms/overview`.
- Confirm the company name, role, and entitlement badge are correct.
- Review the onboarding checklist on the PMS overview page.

## Step 2: Configure staff and scopes

- Add managers, accountants, maintenance coordinators, agents, and viewers.
- Assign property scope for branch or portfolio teams.
- Keep at least two trusted users as owner/manager for continuity.

## Step 3: Add or import core data

For small portfolios, create records manually:

1. PMS properties
2. Units
3. Tenants
4. Leases with rent schedule generation

For larger portfolios, use `/pms/import-export`:

- Download the CSV templates.
- Preview imports first.
- Fix invalid rows.
- Commit only validated rows.
- Export the rent roll and compare it with the company source file.

## Step 4: Configure operations

- Add policies and notices.
- Create rent, overdue, lease-expiry, and maintenance communication templates.
- Add vendors/contractors and trade categories.
- Upload or link lease agreements, ID documents, inspection reports, and maintenance invoices.

## Step 5: Validate finance

- Record one manual rent payment.
- Open the printable receipt.
- Review accounting ledger entries.
- Open owner/property statement preview.
- Export rent roll and accounting summary CSVs.

## Step 6: Validate tenant portal

- Ask the tenant to create a regular lux.om account.
- Link the user email from the PMS tenant record.
- Confirm the tenant sees only their lease, rent dues, maintenance requests, and documents.

## Step 7: Launch day checks

- No staff member has broader access than needed.
- No tenant can access another tenant's data.
- All open maintenance requests have status, priority, and target date.
- Documents with expiry dates are visible in expiry alerts.
- Import/export and report pages load on desktop and mobile.
